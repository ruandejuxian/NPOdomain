Common Questions

Is it really free?
Yes! The subdomain (e.g., you.npo.io.vn) is 100% free forever for registered non-profits.
Can I use it with Wix/Squarespace?
Yes. We provide full DNS records (A, CNAME, TXT) so you can point the domain to any service provider.
Why "npo.io.vn"?
It is our main identity domain. By using a subdomain, you inherit our domain authority and security infrastructure without the cost.
